Frequently Asked Qustions

You can easily create an account by clicking on the register button located on the top right of our navigation bar. You will be redirected to a registration page where you will be prompted to enter a username, password, and details about yourself like your name, address, contact info, etc. Once you have entered these details, you can now use your credentials to log in into our marketplace and proceed to your shopping.

Please do note however, that details such as your phone number and email address are needed to be as accurate as possible, as we utilize these details to serve you as efficiently as possible. Inaccurate details may hinder us from processing and delivering your orders on time.

You can place an order by pressing the "Checkout" tab in the navigation bar or by clicking the basket icon on the top right corner of the page and pressing the "Proceed to Checkout" button. You will be given a summary of your order and a shipping form that you will have to fill up with the address where your order should be sent.

Once the shipping form is filled up, you can now press the "Place Order" button and a notification will be shown that confirms that you have succesfully placed your order. We will be contacting you 12-24 hours after your order has been posted either via your email or phone number to confirm your order.

As always, please make sure that address in your shipping form is as accurate as possible. This helps us save time in delivering your order, thus making your order arrive on time.

You will be paying for your order once our logistics team has delivered the order to your door. The logistics team will be the one responsible for collecting your payment and giving you your physical reciept.

Payments collected by or logistics team will be reflected to your account 6-12 hours after your payment.
